Executive Summary

Not more AI tools.
One operating system.

Laqshya is one of India's leading integrated media and experiential groups, working across media planning, owned assets, advertising, live experiences, creative technology and digital. As it scales, workflows lean increasingly on manual coordination and knowledge held in silos.

The gap today

AI in silos

Teams have started exploring AI on their own, but there is no unified operating system for knowledge sharing, workflow standardisation or governed, enterprise-wide automation. An estimated fewer than 50 of 350+ people actively use AI.

The objective

First in India

Establish a structured AI Operating System that lifts productivity, accelerates workflows and enables governed adoption at scale, positioning Laqshya as the first Indian media group with a fully operational enterprise AI backbone. The benchmark is WPP Open, globally.

This proposal covers 14 requirements across 4 strategic pillars: Knowledge & Enablement, Core Operations, Growth & Creative, and Enterprise Expansion.
